QorTroller — Core Controllers of their gaming data.

The reference implementation of V.A.P.I. — Verifiable Autonomous Physical Intelligence — a coined Decentralized Physical Infrastructure (DePIN) sub-category for protocols where the physical-input source is also the cryptographic agency-holder over the data those physical interactions generate. In QorTroller's case: gamers and their controllers, producing data, owning that data. Built native to IoTeX's Internet of Trusted Things foundation; composable as a single on-chain call.

HUMANITY PROVEN GAMER-SOVEREIGN ANCHORED ON IoTeX
01

What QorTroller is

A cryptographic anti-cheat protocol for competitive console gaming. Instead of policing players after the fact, QorTroller cryptographically proves a real human is on a real, attested controller — and gives that gamer sovereignty over the data those physical interactions generate.

Cheating doesn't need to be punished — it can't exist when humanity is cryptographically proven and the gamer retains sovereignty.
Proven, not surveilled

The gamer's humanity is honored and proven by physics + math — not by spyware. No keystroke harvesting, no kernel anti-cheat.

Sovereign by design

The physical-input source holds the keys. Consent is granted and revoked by the gamer's own wallet — the bridge can only observe.

Composable proof

Eligibility resolves to a single on-chain view call. Other protocols can gate on QorTroller without trusting QorTroller's servers.

03

Proof of Autonomous Cognition

Every cognition cycle emits a 228-byte PoAC record — a 164-byte signed body plus a 64-byte ECDSA-P256 signature. Records chain by hashing the body only.

164signed body
64ECDSA-P256 sig
chain_link_hash = SHA-256( raw[0:164] ) — the 164-byte body only, never the full 228 bytes. Wire format is FROZEN.

11

How it functions going forward

The roadmap deepens the physical-evidence surface and widens composability — every step gated on empirical evidence, never shipped on a calendar. The newest layer — presence — is a novel inhabitant on the existing foundation: it touches no FROZEN-v1 primitive, no PoAC wire format, no chain, no contract.

Validated on the foundation · 2026-05 · on-rig, negative-controlled, Mythos-gated

PoEP · Proof of Embodied Presence · VALIDATED

Proves a live human on the certified Edge now — the claim the protocol actually needs, distinct from sub-grade identity. Liveness reflex band + adaptive-trigger device-auth + nonce freshness, folded into one born-PQ commitment. Reflex band validated across 5 players; activates only at N≥50 per player + governance.

Adaptive-trigger device-auth · NOVEL

No known production prior art: physical-control-response as a device-auth primitive. A real Edge reshapes its force-response under adaptive-trigger resistance; an emulator, Cronus, or XIM renders no such physics — so it fails. The embodied differentiator, not just "a human is present."

L9 · Proof of Causal Presence · BANKED

Input causally drives the rendered camera motion at a human reaction lag; the link collapses under a time-shuffle control and is robust to 400–500 ms cloud streams — closing the cloud-gaming-bot stealth gap from the input side.

BCC · Behavioral Capture Chain · DORMANT

Casual play grows a provenance-chained corpus, harvesting only causally-PRESENT sessions — self-cleaning, isolated, structurally unable to mutate a proven number. The engine that closes the one open gap: breadth.
