QorTroller — Core Controllers of their gaming data.

The reference implementation of V.A.P.I. — Verifiable Autonomous Physical Intelligence — a coined Decentralized Physical Infrastructure (DePIN) sub-category for protocols where the physical-input source is also the cryptographic agency-holder over the data those physical interactions generate. In QorTroller's case: gamers and their controllers, producing data, owning that data. Built native to IoTeX's Internet of Trusted Things foundation; composable as a single on-chain call.

HUMANITY PROVEN GAMER-SOVEREIGN ANCHORED ON IoTeX
01

What QorTroller is

A cryptographic anti-cheat protocol for competitive console gaming. Instead of policing players after the fact, QorTroller cryptographically proves a real human is on a real, attested controller — and gives that gamer sovereignty over the data those physical interactions generate.

Cheating doesn't need to be punished — it can't exist when humanity is cryptographically proven and the gamer retains sovereignty.
Proven, not surveilled

The gamer's humanity is honored and proven by physics + math — not by spyware. No keystroke harvesting, no kernel anti-cheat.

Sovereign by design

The physical-input source holds the keys. Consent is granted and revoked by the gamer's own wallet — the bridge can only observe.

Composable proof

Eligibility resolves to a single on-chain view call. Other protocols can gate on QorTroller without trusting QorTroller's servers.

Every cognition cycle emits a 228-byte PoAC record — a 164-byte signed body plus a 64-byte ECDSA-P256 signature. Records chain by hashing the body only.

Tech-stack pillars · live on testnet

What the protocol provides — described as engineering surfaces, not as project phases. Each pillar has an on-chain artifact, a code path, and an in-CI test.

ZK proofs of human authorship

A Groth16 verifier wrapper on IoTeX — 0x5182372d… — accepts per-session zero-knowledge proofs that gameplay came from a real human above a disclosed humanity floor. The public input sanitizedTraceRoot binds the proof to a sanitized macro-intent matrix (60 Hz, 4-bit quantized) — biometric features are wiped by the FORBIDDEN_COLUMNS frozenset (INV-VHR-004) before the matrix is hashed.

L1 block-hash anchored recency

An on-chain registry — 0x96244031…, LIVE 2026-06-05 — binds every session's open + close beacons to real IoTeX L1 block hashes at a FROZEN cadence (ANCHOR_CADENCE = 64, INV-TBR-002). Stale re-listing, pre-computation drips, and tournament-window backdating are detected at the freshness floor. Honest dormant fallback when keeper unset.

W3bstream Wasm compute sandbox

A Rust applet under w3bstream/applet/ compiles to wasm32-unknown-unknown. The sandbox manifest is FROZEN-pinned: frame_grabbing=false, optical_capture=false, blockhash_temporal_rules=true. The applet validates ingestion mechanically (cadence + isolation + non-zero PQ commitment) and pins INV-W3S-001/002/005 enforced fail-closed in CI.

Silicon-rooted hardware identity

Per-device birth certificates anchored at VAPIManufacturerDeviceRegistry at manufacture time. deviceId = keccak256(controller_pubkey) FROZEN across the entire protocol. IoTeX's P256 precompile at 0x0100 verifies the cert in one view-call. VAPIProtocolLensV2.isFullyEligible_PathA(bytes32) gates tournaments + marketplace + export in a single composable read.

Gamer-sovereign consent surface

A standalone dApp at /consent · Wallet signs every grant + revoke · Bridge can READ but NEVER WRITE · contract enforces msg.sender == gamer · first on-chain manifest write recorded tx 0xd02c051e…20bd block 44354567 with allowReplayProofs=true · append-only consent_event_log table records grant → revoke → re-grant as 3 distinct rows · regulator-facing immutable audit trail.

How it functions going forward

The roadmap deepens the physical-evidence surface and widens composability — every step gated on empirical evidence, never shipped on a calendar. The newest layer — presence — is a novel inhabitant on the existing foundation: it touches no FROZEN-v1 primitive, no PoAC wire format, no chain, no contract.

Validated on the foundation · 2026-05 · on-rig, negative-controlled, Mythos-gated

PoEP · Proof of Embodied Presence · VALIDATED

Proves a live human on the certified Edge now — the claim the protocol actually needs, distinct from sub-grade identity. Liveness reflex band + adaptive-trigger device-auth + nonce freshness, folded into one born-PQ commitment. Reflex band validated across 5 players; activates only at N≥50 per player + governance.

Adaptive-trigger device-auth · NOVEL

No known production prior art: physical-control-response as a device-auth primitive. A real Edge reshapes its force-response under adaptive-trigger resistance; an emulator, Cronus, or XIM renders no such physics — so it fails. The embodied differentiator, not just "a human is present."

L9 · Proof of Causal Presence · BANKED

Input causally drives the rendered camera motion at a human reaction lag; the link collapses under a time-shuffle control and is robust to 400–500 ms cloud streams — closing the cloud-gaming-bot stealth gap from the input side.

BCC · Behavioral Capture Chain · DORMANT

Casual play grows a provenance-chained corpus, harvesting only causally-PRESENT sessions — self-cleaning, isolated, structurally unable to mutate a proven number. The engine that closes the one open gap: breadth.

Still forward · activation · hardening · hardware · aligned to IoTeX IIP-64 (PR #72)

PoEP activation · born-PQ credential

On-chain commitment registration + a born-PQ device credential aligned to IoTeX IIP-64 (Draft, PR #72). Per its device-class tiering, the long-lived controller identity targets SLH-DSA-128s / ML-DSA-44 (§4.6), not consensus-tier ML-DSA-65. The SHA-256 commitment is post-quantum-safe today; the device key rotates at VHP renewal — QorTroller's analog of IIP-64 §4.8.5 iPACT-DePIN. Honest born-PQ, by design.

PoAC-v2 · Groth16 → STARK

Migrate the zero-knowledge layer Groth16 → a hash-based STARK for a post-quantum verification horizon — aligning with IIP-64's SP1, STARK-native choice that avoids the quantum-vulnerable Groth16 recursion step.

Controller-firmware reference

Root the adaptive-trigger device-auth + born-PQ identity in the controller's secure element, provisioned at manufacture — a reference implementation of IIP-64 §4.6 (DePIN device provisioning) + §4.8.5 (iPACT-DePIN). QorTroller as a device standard for IoTeX, not just an app on one.

Token issuance

Gated, by hard rule, on a per-player separation ratio > 1.0 — economic defensibility before any TGE. Honesty over hype.

Published negative — the behavioral-lattice (GCAP) catches more adversaries than even a strong Mahalanobis baseline, but rejects too many real humans (acceptance 0.81 → 0.58): data-limited, not a primitive. We publish what doesn't work, because a trust protocol that hides its negatives isn't one.
