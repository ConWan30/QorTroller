## Summary

Phase 227 extended the ProtocolCoherenceRegistry contract to anchor the
`governance_provenance_hash` on IoTeX L1 alongside the fleet Merkle root. Each coherence
cycle now carries a cryptographic link between the on-chain anchor and the governance event
chain, enabling GOVERNANCE_PROVENANCE_ANCHOR_DRIFT detection when the off-chain
governance_provenance_chain is tampered without updating the on-chain anchor.

**INV-013 note:** Phase 227 introduced `anchor_coherence_with_provenance()` to chain.py.
This caused INV-013 digest drift (resolved in commit `29d5520c`, 2026-04-18).
